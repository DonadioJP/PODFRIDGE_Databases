    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success

    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 10,194,686 offender profiles and 395,105  forensic profiles as of September 2011. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of September 2011, CODIS has produced over 161,100 hits assisting in more than 155,100 investigations.
