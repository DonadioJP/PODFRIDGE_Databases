Ms. Murphy,

In response to your Public Records request concerning the demographic characteristics of the Indiana SDIS DNA database, the following are the responses to your request, in the most detail I could query in our system. While the DNA profiles themselves are labeled convicted offender versus arrestee, the database housing the other information you are seeking is not divided in that way so the percentages listed are for the combined categories. These statistics are as of today's date and are approximate. Race and Ethnicity are self-reported.

  1. Total DNA Profiles:
    Convicted Offender profiles = 279,654
    Arrestee profiles = 21,087
  2. Gender Distribution:
    Female = 20%
    Male = 80%
  3. Racial/Ethnic Distribution:
    Caucasian = 70%
    Black = 26%
    Hispanic = 4%
    All other categories are so small that together they represent less than 1%
