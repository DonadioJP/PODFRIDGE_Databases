    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success

    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 10,061,069 offender profiles and 388,979 forensic profiles as of August 2011. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of August 2011, CODIS has produced over 158,200 hits assisting in more than 152,500 investigations.
