    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 9,110,007 offender profiles and 346,613 forensic profiles as of November 2010. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of November 2010, CODIS has produced over 130,900 hits assisting in more than 127,600 investigations.
