CODIS - NDIS Statistics

Measuring Success


The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 14,117,945 offender1 profiles, 3,864,498 arrestee profiles and 998,241 forensic profiles as of January 2020. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of January 2020, CODIS has produced over 496,129 hits assisting in more than 485,430 investigations.

1Offender profiles include Convicted Offender, Detainee, and Legal profiles at NDIS.

Note: Statistics are available on the map pins and in the tables below for all 50 states and Puerto Rico, as well as for the DC/FBI Lab, District of Columbia (DC), and U.S. Army. (Statistics for the latter three can be found on the Washington, D.C. map pin.)
