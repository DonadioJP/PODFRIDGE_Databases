The Nevada Department of Public Safety has located the responsive information as follows:

It is almost impossible to get the DNA for just the last year because we do not store when the DNA was collected. The DNA lab does not provide us with that date nor do we have a location in the database to store that information. We only store if there is DNA on the individual. So, these are the totals in our entire Nevada criminal history record database. If you need anything else let me know.
