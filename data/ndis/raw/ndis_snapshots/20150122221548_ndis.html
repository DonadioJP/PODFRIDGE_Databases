    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 11,261,417 offender1 profiles, 2,095,147 arrestee profiles and 595,473 forensic profiles as of November 2014. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of November 2014, CODIS has produced over 266,897 hits assisting in more than 255,153 investigations.
