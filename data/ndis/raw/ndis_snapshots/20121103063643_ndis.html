    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 9,930,700 offender1 profiles, 1,245,700 arrestee profiles and 451,900 forensic profiles as of September 2012. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of September 2012, CODIS has produced over 190,500 hits assisting in more than 182,800 investigations.
