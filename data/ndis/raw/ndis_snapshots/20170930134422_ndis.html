CODIS - NDIS Statistics

Measuring Success

The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 13,002,732 offender1 profiles, 2,829,260 arrestee profiles, and 799,021 forensic profiles as of August 2017. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of August 2017, CODIS has produced over 389,494 hits assisting in more than 374,346 investigations.

1Offender profiles include Convicted Offender, Detainee, and Legal profiles at NDIS.

Note: Statistics are available on the map pins and in the tables below for all 50 states and Puerto Rico, as well as for D.C./FBI Lab, D.C./Metro PD, and the U.S. Army. (Statistics for the latter three can be found on the Washington, D.C. map pin.)
