    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 12,205,768 offender1 profiles, 2,258,693 arrestee profiles and 684,519 forensic profiles as of February 2016. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of February 2016, CODIS has produced over 322,011 hits assisting in more than 309,614 investigations.
