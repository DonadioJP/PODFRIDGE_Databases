CODIS - NDIS Statistics

Measuring Success 

The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 12,689,529 offender1 profiles, 2,583,427 arrestee profiles and 750,217 forensic profiles as of December 2016. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of December 2016, CODIS has produced over 359,077 hits assisting in more than 344,194 investigations.

1Offender profiles include Convicted Offender, Detainee, and Legal profiles at NDIS.

Note: Statistics are available on the map pins and in the tables below for all 50 states and Puerto Rico, as well as for D.C./FBI Lab, D.C./Metro PD, and the U.S. Army. (Statistics for the latter three can be found on the Washington, D.C. map pin.)
