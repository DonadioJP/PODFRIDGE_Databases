    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 10,142,600 offender1 profiles, 1,362,800 arrestee profiles and 472,500 forensic profiles as of January 2013. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of January 2013, CODIS has produced over 200,300 hits assisting in more than 192,400 investigations.
