CODIS - NDIS Statistics

Measuring Success


The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 12,854,420 offender1 profiles, 2,708,809 arrestee profiles and 772,613 forensic profiles as of April 2017. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of April 2017, CODIS has produced over 373,099 hits assisting in more than 358,069 investigations.

1Offender profiles include Convicted Offender, Detainee, and Legal profiles at NDIS.

Note: Statistics are available on the map pins and in the tables below for all 50 states and Puerto Rico, as well as for D.C./FBI Lab, D.C./Metro PD, and the U.S. Army. (Statistics for the latter three can be found on the Washington, D.C. map pin.)
