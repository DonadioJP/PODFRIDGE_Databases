    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 9,716,400 offender1 profiles, 1,111,300 arrestee profiles and 432,300 forensic profiles as of May 2012. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of May 2012, CODIS has produced over 180,400 hits assisting in more than 173,000 investigations.
