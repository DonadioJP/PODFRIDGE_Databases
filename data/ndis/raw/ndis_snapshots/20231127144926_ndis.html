CODIS-NDIS Statistics

Measuring Success

The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 16,532,335 offender1 profiles, 5,190,279 arrestee profiles and 1,282,418 forensic profiles as of August 2023. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of August 2023, CODIS has produced over 674,405 hits assisting in more than 656,893 investigations.

1Offender profiles include Convicted Offender, Detainee, and Legal profiles at NDIS.

Note: Statistics are available on the map pins and in the tables below for all 50 states and Puerto Rico, as well as for the DC/FBI Lab, District of Columbia (DC), and U.S. Army. (Statistics for the latter three can be found on the Washington, D.C. map pin.)
