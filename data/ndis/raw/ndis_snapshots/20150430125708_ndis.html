    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 11,685,829 offender1 profiles, 1,944,423 arrestee profiles and 619,280 forensic profiles as of March 2015. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of March 2015, CODIS has produced over 280,451 hits assisting in more than 267,461 investigations.
