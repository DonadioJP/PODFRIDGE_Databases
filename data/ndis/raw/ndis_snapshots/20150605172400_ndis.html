    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 11,736,960 offender1 profiles, 1,973,831 arrestee profiles and 625,370 forensic profiles as of April 2015. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of April 2015, CODIS has produced over 283,440 hits assisting in more than 270,211 investigations.
