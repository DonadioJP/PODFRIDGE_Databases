    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 12,069,150 offender1 profiles, 2,173,094 arrestee profiles and 668,285 forensic profiles as of November 2015. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of November 2015, CODIS has produced over 311,248 hits assisting in more than 299,130 investigations.
