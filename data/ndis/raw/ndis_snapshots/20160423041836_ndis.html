    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 12,253,681 offender1 profiles, 2,292,478 arrestee profiles and 690,220 forensic profiles as of March 2016. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of March 2016, CODIS has produced over 325,615 hits assisting in more than 313,278 investigations.
