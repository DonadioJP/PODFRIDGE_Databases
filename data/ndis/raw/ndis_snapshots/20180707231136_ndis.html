CODIS - NDIS Statistics

Measuring Success


The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 13,369,976 offender1 profiles, 3,136,528 arrestee profiles, and 858,321 forensic profiles as of May 2018. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of May 2018, CODIS has produced over 422,690 hits assisting in more than 406,669 investigations.

1Offender profiles include Convicted Offender, Detainee, and Legal profiles at NDIS.

Note: Statistics are available on the map pins and in the tables below for all 50 states and Puerto Rico, as well as for D.C./FBI Lab, D.C./Metro PD, and the U.S. Army. (Statistics for the latter three can be found on the Washington, D.C. map pin.)
